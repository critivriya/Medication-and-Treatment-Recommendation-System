Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

We developed a web app using Flask and a Decision Tree Classifier model to help users identify potential illnesses based on symptoms they report.

The Problem

People often struggle to differentiate between diseases when symptoms overlap. Our goal was to provide a quick, easy solution for users to identify potential health issues.

The Solution

The app allows users to input their symptoms, and using a trained model, it predicts the most likely disease. Additional information like disease descriptions, precautions, medications, and diet tips are also provided.

Key Features

  • User-friendly interface for easy symptom input.
  • Accurate disease predictions using machine learning.
  • Comprehensive information about predicted diseases.

Conclusion

Our web app makes healthcare information more accessible and empowers users to make informed decisions about their health. It represents a step toward better, more accessible healthcare through technology.
